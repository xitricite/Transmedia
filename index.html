Facts

  • Conspiracy coverage? - Not very well covered
    • Australian Maritime Sea Authority cover up?
    • ALA - increased toxcicity 50%
    • PTTEP - Sold for too cheap (load recieved 270 million, yet sold for 270 million?)
  • Under reported compared to BP oil spill (Gulf of Mexico). Too many questions not asked
